# Other Platform Closures - Secondary Analysis

## Confirmed Closures

### Async Art
- **Status**: Stopped operations
- **Date**: October 2023
- **Type**: Programmable/evolving NFT art platform
- **Notes**: Early innovator in "dynamic" NFTs (art that changes over time)

### MakersPlace
- **Status**: Closed
- **Date**: January 2025
- **Type**: Digital art marketplace
- **Notes**: Less public documentation available

### KnownOrigin
- **Status**: Closed
- **Date**: July 2024
- **Type**: Digital art marketplace
- **Acquisition**: Bought by eBay in 2022
- **Notes**: Corporate acquisition didn't prevent shutdown 2 years later

### Christie's Digital Art Department
- **Status**: Closed
- **Date**: September 2025
- **Type**: Major auction house's digital art initiative
- **Notes**: Traditional art institution exiting cryptoart space

### Additional 2025 Closures
- **Kraken NFT**: Ended services
- **Bybit NFT**: Ended services
- **X2Y2**: Ended services
- **LG Art Lab**: Ended services

## Sotheby's Note
