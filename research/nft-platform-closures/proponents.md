# Creator Coins - Proponents & Defenders

This file documents the arguments, success stories, and key figures defending Zora's creator coin model and Base's integration.

---

## Core Proponent Arguments

### For Creators

- **Direct monetization without intermediaries**: Creators sell tokens/content directly to fans
- **Ongoing royalties**: Smart contracts ensure revenue from resales
- **Earn from engagement**: 1% fee share from every trade
- **Front-loaded supply**: 10M tokens upfront + 50% streamed over 5 years
- **Dynamic pricing**: Creators set start/max prices, community trading adjusts value
- **Low barriers**: <$0.50 minting costs

### For Supporters

- **"Bet on creators"**: Direct financial backing shows support
- **Ownership stake**: Tokens grant exposure to creator's success
- **Exclusive access**: Tokens grant premium content, events, products, services
- **Community voting**: DAO-like decision rights
- **Potential appreciation**: Tokens may gain value with creator's growth

### For Platform Model

- **Permissionless**: Open-source protocol where anyone can build, mint, create marketplaces
- **Creator ownership**: "Zora puts ownership and control back into the hands of creators"
- **Transparency & permanence**: Every listing/mint/bid recorded fully onchain
- **Zero intermediaries**: Permissionless trading prioritizes privacy
- **Distinct from speculation**: Focuses on utility-driven token models over pure speculation

## Key Proponent Figures

### 1. Jacob Horne (Zora Co-Founder/CEO)

**Background:**
- Joined Coinbase January 2017, left after 3.5 years to start Zora
- Zora co-founder and CEO
- Coinbase product development experience

**Core arguments:**
- Addresses "systemic issues in traditional systems" that are "outdated (rooted in 1600s structures)"
- **Dynamic pricing for creator products**: Creators set start and max prices; community buying/selling adjusts price
- **Creator coins as monetization**: Every post becomes a coin paired with creator's coin; creators earn 1% of trades and 50% of their coin supply over 5 years
- **Liquidity and royalties via AMMs**: Uses single-sided liquidity pools where creators provide initial LP, earning fees as trading volume grows
- **Feedback loop**: More creators produce more content, driving trades, attention, and users, while routing value back to creators and early supporters

**Quote (paraphrased):** "We've built a sustainable creator economy where creators can direct their own projects and control their own distribution channels."

## Critical Nuance

### Vitalik Buterin is NOT a Supporter

**Important**: Despite some coverage suggesting endorsement, Vitalik actually **criticizes** Zora's creator coin model:

- **Core criticism**: "Market outcomes still largely reflect existing social capital rather than sustained creative contribution"
- **Problem not solved**: Even with improved infrastructure, model "doesn't effectively discover or elevate quality creators based on merit alone"
- **Alternative proposal**: Curated DAOs (~200 members) as quality filters + prediction markets betting on DAO curation decisions
- **Fundamental critique**: Platforms like Zora remain "optimized for price discovery, not cultural or intellectual judgment," leaving them "vulnerable to hype-driven volatility rather than sustained creator value"
