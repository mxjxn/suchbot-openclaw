# Zora - Controversy Analysis

## Platform Status
**Still operating** (as of early 2026), but facing major community backlash

## Key Controversies

### 1. Account Suspensions (2024-2025)
**#ZoraScam Campaign**

- **September 2025**: $Kazonomics token account suspended
  - Shifted to "sell-only" mode
  - Erased value for **4,200+ holders** (nearly half active user base)
  - No warning or clear justification given

- **November 2024**: Fake NFT collection accounts suspended
  - No ETH refunds issued
  - Unilateral platform decision

- **Tyson Fury case**: Celebrity-linked account suspended, tied to alleged fake promotions

**Criticism**: "Centralized control in disguise" - app-level Terms of Service allow bans at Zora's "sole discretion"

### 2. ZORA Token Airdrop Scandal (April 2025)
**Major backlash from community**

**Issues raised:**
- **Zero utility**: No governance rights, no equity, no platform role. Team called it "for fun"
- **Insider-heavy allocation**: 65% to team and investors (including Coinbase Ventures)
- **Poor execution**:
  - Users reported $258 gas fees for $0.99 in tokens (negative ROI)
  - "Ghost allocations" to inactive wallets
  - Pre-airdrop team sales via exchanges (Bybit, KuCoin)

**Market impact:**
- ZORA price dropped **over 50%** post-launch
- Trading volume: $31M â†’ $9M in 48 hours
- Active users banned for "excessive" referral activity

### 3. NFT Pivot / Abandonment
- **Abruptly ended ERC-721 NFT support** in favor of ERC-20-like memecoins
- Frustrated creators who built on Zora's original NFT infrastructure

### 4. Pre-token Marketing Allegations
- Accusations of shady marketing with Coinbase/Base
- Insider trading allegations regarding pre-airdrop team sales
