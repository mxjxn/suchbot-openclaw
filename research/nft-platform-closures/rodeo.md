## Migration Tools
Rodeo providing:
1. **Arweave migration tools** - Transfer NFT media and metadata to Arweave (blockchain-based storage)
2. **Migration assistance tool** - Guide asset transfers from platform smart contracts

This is notable: Platform investing in permanent, decentralized storage migration vs. forcing withdrawals to centralized wallets.

## Why Shutdown?
Official: "Did not grow to scale that was sustainable over long term"

Despite strong community engagement, platform couldn't achieve sustainable scale.
