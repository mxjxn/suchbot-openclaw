# Nifty Gateway - Shutdown Analysis

## Platform Status
**Shutting down February 23, 2026**

- Entered **withdrawal-only mode**: January 23, 2026
- **Complete shutdown**: February 23, 2026 (11:59 PM UTC)
- After shutdown: No listing, purchasing, bidding, or selling possible
- Withdrawals remain possible via Gemini Wallet

## Background
- **Founded**: 2018 (some sources say 2020)
- **Acquired**: By Gemini in 2019
- **Peak GMV**: $300 million in 2021
- **Total transactions**: $1.2B+ over platform lifetime
- **Notable sale**: Beeple's $69.3M Christie's sale helped drive hype (though not directly on Nifty)

## Shutdown Announcement
Gemini announced closure to refocus on building a "one-stop super app." Instructions emailed to affected users for migrating assets to Gemini ecosystem.

## Migration Requirements
Users must withdraw:
- ~650,000 NFTs
- USD balances
- ETH balances

**Deadline**: February 23, 2026

After deadline: Withdrawals only possible through Gemini Wallet.

## Arweave Migration (Post-Shutdown)
- Nifty Gateway moved metadata to Arweave
- **Critical limitation**: Some older NFTs cannot migrate
- Even with Arweave: files only persist if users continue paying to pin them
- When platforms shut down, metadata links can disappear
- When hosting costs go unpaid, artworks may become inaccessible

## Historical Precedent
- Artists like XCOPY lost early works when platforms (Ascribe, Digital Objects) closed
- Collectors now refer to these as "lost XCOPYs"
- Shows real risk of art loss due to platform dependencies

## Why Shutdown?
Official reason: Gemini refocusing on exchange and wallet services, broader "super app" vision.

Unstated factors (speculative):
- Market volume collapse (art NFTs down 93%)
- Unsustainable speculative model
- Centralized platform overhead vs decentralized alternatives
